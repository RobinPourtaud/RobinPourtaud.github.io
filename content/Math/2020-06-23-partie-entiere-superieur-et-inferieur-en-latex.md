Cet article présentera l&rsquo;utilisation de la partie entière, aussi bien supérieure qu&rsquo;inférieure en Latex. Deux macros seront explicitées pour faciliter leur utilisation. 

## Partie entière supérieure en LaTex :



Pour prendre la partie entière supérieure d&rsquo;un entier<img loading="lazy" src="https://keskec.fr/wp-content/ql-cache/quicklatex.com-fbe378ec22a7dced7d0ff7374edd698a_l3.svg" class="ql-img-inline-formula " alt="&#120;" title="Rendered by QuickLaTeX.com" height="9" width="11" style="vertical-align: 0px;" /> en Latex, il suffit de taper ceci : 

<pre class="wp-block-code" aria-describedby="shcb-language-93" data-shcb-language-name="TeX" data-shcb-language-slug="tex"><div>
  <code class="hljs language-tex">&lt;span class="hljs-tag">\&lt;span class="hljs-name">lceil&lt;/span>&lt;/span> x &lt;span class="hljs-tag">\&lt;span class="hljs-name">rceil&lt;/span>&lt;/span> </code>
</div>

<small class="shcb-language" id="shcb-language-93"><span class="shcb-language__label">Code language:</span> <span class="shcb-language__name">TeX</span> <span class="shcb-language__paren">(</span><span class="shcb-language__slug">tex</span><span class="shcb-language__paren">)</span></small></pre>

Voici le résultat : 

<p class="has-text-align-center">
  <img loading="lazy" src="https://keskec.fr/wp-content/ql-cache/quicklatex.com-77d4074048fb4a6ab2639ea2776f09a7_l3.svg" class="ql-img-inline-formula " alt="&#92;&#108;&#99;&#101;&#105;&#108;&#32;&#120;&#32;&#92;&#114;&#99;&#101;&#105;&#108;" title="Rendered by QuickLaTeX.com" height="30" width="33" style="vertical-align: -8px;" />
</p>

## Partie entière inférieure en LaTex : 

La partie entière inférieure en Latex d&rsquo;un entier<img loading="lazy" src="https://keskec.fr/wp-content/ql-cache/quicklatex.com-fbe378ec22a7dced7d0ff7374edd698a_l3.svg" class="ql-img-inline-formula " alt="&#120;" title="Rendered by QuickLaTeX.com" height="9" width="11" style="vertical-align: 0px;" /> se fait de façon analogue à celle supérieure : 

<pre class="wp-block-code" aria-describedby="shcb-language-94" data-shcb-language-name="TeX" data-shcb-language-slug="tex"><div>
  <code class="hljs language-tex">&lt;span class="hljs-tag">\&lt;span class="hljs-name">lfloor&lt;/span>&lt;/span> x &lt;span class="hljs-tag">\&lt;span class="hljs-name">rfloor&lt;/span>&lt;/span> </code>
</div>

Voilà son rendu :

</p>

## Raccourcir son utilisation avec une macro? 

Il est possible de faire une macro de la partie entière afin de simplifier son utilisation : 

<pre class="wp-block-code" aria-describedby="shcb-language-95" data-shcb-language-name="TeX" data-shcb-language-slug="tex"><div>
  <code class="hljs language-tex">&lt;span class="hljs-tag">\&lt;span class="hljs-name">newcommand&lt;/span>&lt;span class="hljs-string">{\floor}&lt;/span>&lt;span class="hljs-string">[1]&lt;/span>&lt;span class="hljs-string">{\lfloor #1 \rfloor}&lt;/span>&lt;/span>
  &lt;span class="hljs-tag">\&lt;span class="hljs-name">newcommand&lt;/span>&lt;span class="hljs-string">{\ceil}&lt;/span>&lt;span class="hljs-string">[1]&lt;/span>&lt;span class="hljs-string">{\lceil #1 \rceil}&lt;/span>&lt;/span></code>
</div>

<small class="shcb-language" id="shcb-language-95"><span class="shcb-language__label">Code language:</span> <span class="shcb-language__name">TeX</span> <span class="shcb-language__paren">(</span><span class="shcb-language__slug">tex</span><span class="shcb-language__paren">)</span></small></pre>

Il est ainsi possible d&rsquo;utiliser : 

<pre class="wp-block-code" aria-describedby="shcb-language-96" data-shcb-language-name="TeX" data-shcb-language-slug="tex"><div>
  <code class="hljs language-tex">&lt;span class="hljs-tag">\&lt;span class="hljs-name">floor&lt;/span>&lt;span class="hljs-string">{x}&lt;/span>&lt;/span>
  &lt;span class="hljs-tag">\&lt;span class="hljs-name">ceil&lt;/span>&lt;span class="hljs-string">{x}&lt;/span>&lt;/span></code>
</div>
